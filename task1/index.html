  • 01 How long should an eLearning course be?

    There isn't a simple and unique response for this question. The variables can be many. But as a best practice, most courses should clock in around the 10-15 minute mark and avoid going beyond 30 minutes.

  • 02 How do you evaluate course effectiveness?

    You can measure the effectiveness of eLearning courses in several ways, but it's always an important step. If a course didn't achieve its goal, it means a waste of time, energy and resources

  • 03 Should there be forced navigation in eLearning courses?

    Open navigation instead delivers big time results. By unlocking the navigation and letting users backtrack or skip ahead as they feel necessary, moving past screens of content they already understand or jumping to a particular chapter that they want to review, designers can make the content more accessible and help users feel more engaged.
